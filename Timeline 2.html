Timeline

Below is a timeline of impotant accomplishments and noteworthy events in Hillary Clinton's life:

  • 1947 October 26- Hillary Diane Rodham was born at a hospital in Chicago, IL to Dorothy and Hugh Rodham.

  • 1962 April 15- Hillary has the opportunity to meet Martin Luther King Jr. with her church youth group in Chicago.

  • 1973 May- Hillary recieves her Juris Doctor degree from Yale Law School, where she served on the editorial board of the Yale Review of Law and Social Action.

  • 1980 February 27- Hillary gives birth to her daughter, Chelsea Clinton, who was born at Baptist Medical Center in Little Rock, Arkansas

  • 1992- Hillary husband, Bill Clinton is elected Presidnet of the United States.

  • 2009 January 29- Hillary is nominated by Barack Obama to be the 67th U.S. Secretary of State.
