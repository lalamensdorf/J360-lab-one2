Fun Facts

Below are some fun facts on Hilary Clinton

  • As a young girl, Hillary wasn't overly interested in politics. She instead dreamed of becoming either a baseball player, journalist, or astronaut, according to the National Constitution Center.

  • According to Biography.com, Hillary Rodham was born on Oct. 26, 1947, in Chicago, Ill. to Hugh and Dorothy Rodham. She was raised in Park Ridge, Illinois along with her two younger brothers Hugh Jr. and Anthony.

  • In addition to being the first former First Lady to ever run for President Hillary is also the first presidential spouse to earn a postgraduate degree and the first to be elected to national office according to Biography.com.
